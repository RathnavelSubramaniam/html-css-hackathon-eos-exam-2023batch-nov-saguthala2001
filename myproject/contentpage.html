RVS College of Arts and Science (RVS CAS) is an ISO 9001:2000 certified institute in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India. It is established in the year 1986. The college is affiliated to Bharathiar University, Coimbatore. The campus is located adjacent to the Coimbatore-Trichy highway at Sulur. RVS College of Arts and Science was accorded with autonomous status in 2004 by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and re-accredited in 2014 with 'A' grade. With a vision to impart quality education in the digital era to its students, RVS Coimbatore has been awarded as the fourth-best college in the city.
